Overview

Founded April 2023 in Shenzhen by Dr. Yandong Eric Guo, AI2 Robotics builds general-purpose humanoid robots (AlphaBot series) powered by its proprietary GOVLA (Global & Omni-body Vision-Language-Action) model, branded Alpha Brain. GOVLA is the world's first model enabling 360° perception, whole-body control, and long-horizon task reasoning — a differentiated end-to-end architecture vs. competitors using third-party foundation models. Targets industrial manufacturing: quality inspection, assembly, and logistics across automotive, electronics, semiconductors, and biotech. Opened its FiS-VLA model (June 2025, endorsed by Yann LeCun). Debuted AlphaBot 2 at the BRIDGE Summit in UAE with live autonomous coffee-making and drum performance demos. Launched ZhiCube modular AI service space concept (Dec 2025) signaling expansion beyond pure industrial into commercial robotics-as-a-service.
