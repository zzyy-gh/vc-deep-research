# 11x as a Competitor to AuraSell

11x should be considered a direct competitor in the AI sales agent space. Key points:

- **Product**: 11x builds AI "digital workers" — notably "Alice" (autonomous SDR for outbound prospecting, qualification, and follow-up) and "Mike" (AI phone agent). They position these as full "digital employees" rather than copilots or tools.
- **Funding**: Raised $50M+ in venture funding, making them well-capitalized in the AI GTM category.
- **Target market**: Enterprise sales teams — overlaps with AuraSell's target buyer persona.
- **Competitive angle**: 11x takes an agent-first approach to sales automation. While AuraSell positions as a full AI-native CRM/GTM OS, 11x focuses specifically on replacing human SDRs with autonomous AI agents. This is relevant because:
  1. AuraSell's "agentic prospecting" feature competes directly with Alice.
  2. If 11x captures the SDR automation wedge, they could expand into broader CRM/GTM — a classic wedge-and-expand threat.
  3. 11x adds to the already crowded field of well-funded competitors ($300M+ collectively deployed), strengthening the "too many horses, not enough track" bear case.

**Implication**: 11x should be added to the competitive landscape table in the next research iteration. Their agent-first positioning and significant funding make them a meaningful competitor that the current analysis overlooks.
